    Every November, graduating students from University of Canberra’s Faculty of Arts and Design develop and display their works for the public in the Student Graduate Exhibition - FADx.

    From the 13-15 November 2024, UC’s Building 7 will feature works from disciplines including Arts, Communication & Media, Design, the Built Environment, and UC Brisbane.

    The students featured on this site come from the Design degrees Visual Communications Design, Industrial Design, Interaction Design, and the Arts degree Digital Media.

    Come on down to Building 7 during the exhibition to see the amazing works displayed by UC's FAD students!
