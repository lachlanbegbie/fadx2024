    • ABOUT

    Every November, graduating students from University of Canberra’s Faculty of Arts and Design develop and display their works for the public in the Student Graduate Exhibition - FADx.

    This year, UC’s Building 7 will feature works from disciplines including Arts, Communication & Media, Design, the Built Environment, and UC Brisbane.

    Come on down to Opening Night on the 13th Nov 5pm - 9pm to see the amazing works displayed by UC's FAD students!

    The exhibition will be for public viewing on the 13th and 14th November between 10am - 3pm.

    STUDENT SHOWCASE

    Refresh

    A selection of students this year have supplied a sample of their work to be displayed here on the FADx website. These students are from the Visual Communications Design, Interaction Design, Industrial Design and Digital Media degrees, though all students from across the faculty are featured at FADx. To see more of the work on display from all FAD students, come along to FADx!
